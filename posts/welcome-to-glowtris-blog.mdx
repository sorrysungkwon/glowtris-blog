Hey, welcome! I'm Sung Kwon, a designer building [Glowtris](https://glowtris.com) — a browser-based Tetris game written entirely with AI assistance.

This blog documents the journey: design decisions, engineering challenges, infrastructure lessons, and unfiltered thoughts about what works (and what doesn't) when you build a game by talking to Claude.

## What you'll find here

**Architecture & Performance**  
How we rebuilt the game engine for 60fps stability, optimized rendering with PixiJS, and handled real-time multiplayer leaderboards.

**Design & UX**  
Building a retro aesthetic with modern interactions, emoji-first visual language, and balancing playfulness with clarity.

**Dev process**  
Honest logs about prompt engineering, debugging AI-generated code, and the unconventional workflow of designing + developing + deploying without traditional coding.
